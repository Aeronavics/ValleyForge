====== TM4C123GH6PM ======
Created Friday 09 January 2015

[[[http://www.ti.com/lit/ds/symlink/tm4c123gh6pm.pdf|Datasheet]]][[[http://www.ti.com/product/tm4c123gh6pm|Product Page]]][[[http://www.ti.com/tool/ek-tm4c123gxl|Development Board]]]
The TM4C123GH6PM microcontroller is targeted for industrial applications, including remote monitoring, electronic point-of-sale machines, test and measurement equipment, network appliances and switches, factory automation, HVAC and building control, gaming equipment, motion control, transportation, and fire and security.

**Flash**		256 KB
**EEPROM**	2 KB
**SRAM**		32 KB
**Pin Count**	64
**Frequency**	80 MHz

**Timers**		12
**USART**		8
**SPI**			4
**I2C**			4
**CAN**		2
**ADC**		2x (12 input channels) 12-bit, 1Msps

NOTE: //This target does not currently implement any of the Hardware Abstraction Library. Rather it is configured with the NuttX RTOS.//
