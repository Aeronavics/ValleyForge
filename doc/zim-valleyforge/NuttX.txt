====== NuttX ======

[[NuttX]] is the real-time operating system that the Pixhawk autopilot system runs on. It is designed to comply with POSIX and ANSI standards and runs on a range of different microcontrollers (from 8-bit ATmega cores to 32-bit ARM cores). A complete list of the supported devices can be found [[http://www.nuttx.org/Documentation/NuttX.html#platforms|here]].
